Altai

URL

Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in the world Of Russia. First of all, because of the mountains: if you go Along the ridge, you will see slopes strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open the windows in the car, you will be able to get acquainted with the invisible wonder of these places — the mountain air.

The climate in Altai is temperate, so it is best to go here in summer. So you are  you will see all the diversity of the local flora and fauna. Moose roam the forests of Altai, eagles fly over the ridges, and on the plains Roe deer are grazing. And the famous manuls are also inhabitants of the Altai Territory.
